Indian Institute of Information Technology Nagpur

EST. in 2016

Indian Institute of Information Technology Nagpur is one of the Indian Institutes of Information Technology and an Institute of National Importance located in Nagpur, Maharashtra. The institute started functioning from July 2016. It offers Bachelor of Technology courses in Electronics and Communication Engineering and Computer Science and Engineering. The institute has shifted to its permanent campus, at Waranga,Nagpur.
